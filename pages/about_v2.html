About Me

Hi, I'm Maciek 👋

I'm a PhD student in theoretical and computational neuroscience at the Institute of Science and Technology Austria (ISTA), supervised by Prof. Tim P. Vogels. I've received my Bachelor's degree in Neurobiology from Jagiellonian University in Krakow (Poland) and my Master's degree in Neuroscience through the Neurasmus: Erasmus Mundus Joint Master's Degree in Neuroscience, from three universities: VU Amsterdam (Netherlands), UBordeaux (France), and ULaval (QC, Canada). I am generally interested in how to learn, to remember, and to forget. In my research, I focus on synaptic plasticity and inhibition in spiking neural networks as well as optimization methods for biologically constrained models. Outside of research, I am involved in building a neurocommunity here at ISTA and in Austria. Hobbies are: doomscrolling, badminton, biking, hiking, and drinks at the Donau Kanal after a hot day.
